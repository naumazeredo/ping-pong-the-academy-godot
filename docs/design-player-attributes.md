# Attributes
- Technique
	- Divided between fore and backhand:
		- Control: Placement, Spin, Consistency
		- Offensive: Basic, Drive, Loop, Counter-loop, Smash, Flick
		- Defensive: Block, Active block, Push, Chop/Kato
	- Serve: Spin, Variety, Deception, Accuracy
- Physical
	- Movement
		- Footwork speed
		- Balance
		- Explosiveness
	- Endurance
		- Stamina: how long the player maintains peak performance
		- Recovery rate: how quickly stamina regenerates between points and games
	- Reflex & Perception
		- Reaction time
		- Anticipation: how well the player predicts the shot direction/spin
		- Spin recognition: how accurately the player reads incoming spin
		- Timing: how well timed the player hits the ball
- Mental
	- Competitive traits
		- Motivation
		- Discipline
		- Confidence: boosts offensive success rate
		- Composure: resistance to choking under pressure
		- Clutch factor: performance boost at high-score moments
	- Tactical intelligence
		- Game tactics: shot selection quality
		- Adaptability: ability to adjust to opponent style
		- Focus: reduces performance drops over long rallies
		- Risk management: balances aggressive vs safe play
- Play style
	- Aggression level
	- Rally preference (short vs long)
	- Distance preference (close/mid/far from table)
	- Dominant wing (FH/BH bias)

# Simplified attributes
- Technique
	- Core (divided by fore and back)
		- Strength (attack + control)
		- Looping
		- Blocking
		- Short game (push + flick)
		- Smash
	- Serve
		- Spin
		- Accuracy
		- Deception
- Physical
	- Movement
	- Stamina
	- Reflexes
- Mental
	- Motivation
	- Discipline
	- Confidence
	- Composure (focus + composure + clutch)
	- Game sense (tactics + adaptation)

# Growth Mechanics
- Physical improves slowly but caps higher
- Technical improves with focused drills
- Mental improves mostly from match experience
- Overtraining reduces stamina growth
- High Motivation increases all training gains
- High Discipline reduces random performance drops
